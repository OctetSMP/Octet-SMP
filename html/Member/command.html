Commands

To make gameplay smoother and more community-friendly, Octet SMP provides several /trigger commands.

/trigger TPA

Request to teleport to another player.

1
/trigger TPA- A list of online players appears
2
Click on the player you want to teleport to - Request sent
3
Target clicks "Accept" in chat - Teleported immediately
4
No response within 60 seconds - Request expires

/trigger home

Teleport to your set home location.

1
/trigger sethome- Set your current location as home
2
/trigger home- Teleport back to saved home

/trigger sethome

Set your current location as your home.

1
Go to desired location
2
/trigger sethome- Home saved

/trigger spawn

Teleport to the world spawn point.

1
/trigger spawn- Teleported to world spawn

/trigger lobby

Teleport to the central lobby hub (no mobs, no PvP).

1
/trigger lobby- Teleported to safe lobby area

/trigger shop

Open the server shop interface.

1
/trigger shop- Chest menu opens
2
Click item to buy - Added to inventory, price deducted

/trigger shop_sell

Sell items to the server shop.

1
/trigger shop_sell- Chest menu opens
2
Put items in chest - Balance added automatically

/trigger state

Display your current stats (playtime, kills, deaths, money).

1
/trigger state- Stats appear in chat

Important Notes

  • Commands are case-sensitive. Type them exactly as shown.
  • Abuse of commands (e.g., spamming teleport requests) may result in punishment.
  • These commands are designed to support stable survival gameplay and community interaction.
