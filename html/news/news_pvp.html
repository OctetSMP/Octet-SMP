OCTET SMP — OFFICIAL ANNOUNCEMENT
NEW PVP DIMENSION — NOW AVAILABLE

April 19, 2026

Dear Members,

We are pleased to announce that the new PVP Dimension is officially available for all members.

This dimension has been designed specifically for those who wish to engage in competitive combat with other players, focusing on skill and strategy rather than resource loss.

1. CHALLENGE SYSTEM

You may now use the command "/trigger pvp" to invite another player to a duel.

Upon using the command, a list of online players will appear. You may select any player to challenge.

Once the opponent accepts the invitation, both players will be transported to the PVP Dimension.

The battle will commence immediately. The duel ends when one player is eliminated.

Upon conclusion, both players will be returned to the overworld.

2. EQUIPMENT PRESERVATION

At the start of each battle, all of your items will be automatically saved.

Upon the conclusion of the battle, your items will be restored in full.

You do not face any risk of losing items — including blocks, totems, end crystals, or any other equipment — during combat or upon death.

3. SPECTATOR MODE

You may use the command "/trigger pvp_spec" to observe an ongoing battle.

This will switch you to spectator mode and transport you to the PVP Dimension.

To exit spectator mode, simply type "/trigger pvp_spec" again. You will be returned to survival mode and teleported back to the overworld.

4. RULES

All participants are required to adhere to the following rules:

1. Do not use any command to teleport out of the battle area.

2. Do not leave the designated battle zone until the duel has concluded.

3. Do not use the shop or shop_sell commands during a battle.

4. Do not disconnect from the server during an active battle. Doing so may result in the permanent loss of all items.

5. Do not use "/trigger pvp_accept" and "/trigger pvp_select" randomly. Only leaders are allowed to use those.

5. GET STARTED

We encourage all members to try the new PVP Dimension today.

Challenge your friends using /trigger pvp and experience the thrill of competitive combat in a risk-free environment.

6. REPORTING ISSUES

Should you have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact the Owner or Admin.

This dimension is still in testing period. Therefore if you encounter any bugs, exploits, or technical issues, please report them immediately to the Owner, Admin, or Developer.

Please be reminded that concealment of any bug, exploit, or rule violation — as well as assisting any rule-breaker — will result in disciplinary action.

Thank you for your continued support.

We hope you enjoy this new feature.

Yours sincerely,
Octet SMP Leadership
